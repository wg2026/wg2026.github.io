Program

The conference takes place on 2-4 June 2026, with a welcome reception on the evening of June 1st. On Wednesday 3 June in the afternoon there will be a guided visit to the Omer brewery as social activity followed by the conference diner in the evening, which will take place in the restaurant of Hotel Damier (Grote Markt 41, 8500 Kortrijk).

All talks will take place in lecture room C611, the coffee breaks in the hall of building B, and the lunches in the university restaurant "Alma" (except for lunch on the last day, which will take place in the hall of building A). For a map of the campus, see the Practical Info tab.
