Description

WG conferences aim to connect theory and applications by demonstrating how graph-theoretic concepts can be applied in various areas of computer science. The goal is to present recent results and to identify and explore directions for future research. The 52nd edition of the International Workshop on Graph-Theoretic Concepts in Computer Science (WG 2026) will be held at KU Leuven campus Kulak in Kortrijk (Belgium) from June 2 to 4, 2026, with a welcome reception on the evening of June 1st.
